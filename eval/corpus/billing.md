# Meridian Platform — Billing & Plans

Meridian is a managed data-pipeline platform. Billing is monthly and per workspace.

The Starter plan costs 29 dollars per month. It includes 2 vCPUs, 4 GB of memory, and a
single concurrent pipeline. Starter workspaces are capped at 10 GB of managed storage.

The Team plan costs 99 dollars per month. It includes 8 vCPUs, 16 GB of memory, and up to
five concurrent pipelines. Team workspaces include 100 GB of managed storage and priority
build queues.

The Enterprise plan costs 499 dollars per month. It includes 32 vCPUs, 64 GB of memory,
and unlimited concurrent pipelines. Enterprise adds single sign-on, audit logging, and a
dedicated support channel.

Annual billing is discounted by 20 percent on every plan. Overage storage is billed at
0.10 dollars per gigabyte per month across all plans. Invoices are issued on the first day
of each month and payment is due within 15 days.
