# Meridian Platform — Support & SLA

Support response times depend on the plan and the severity of the issue.

On the Starter plan, support is email only, with a first response within 48 hours. There
is no uptime guarantee on Starter.

On the Team plan, support responds within 4 hours for high-severity issues and within 24
hours for normal issues. The Team plan carries a 99.9 percent monthly uptime guarantee.

On the Enterprise plan, support responds within 1 hour for high-severity issues, 24 hours
a day. The Enterprise plan carries a 99.99 percent monthly uptime guarantee and includes a
named technical account manager.

If a monthly uptime guarantee is missed, affected workspaces receive service credits: 10
percent of the monthly fee for a breach below the guarantee, and 30 percent for uptime
below 99 percent. Credits are applied automatically to the next invoice and never paid as
cash.
